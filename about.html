"we are living in times of Revolutionary change....for which there are no precedents...our children are confronting challenges that are unique in human history"

(Sir Ken Robinson, Out of Our Minds, 3rd Edition, 2017)

For the Public Sector & its partners in Social Purpose, the job of looking ahead, understanding the external landscape, responding to change and moving ahead, has never been more challenging

The nature, pace and scope of current societal change is eye-watering. In business environments influenced by public policy, it can be overwhelming.

But it can also be inspiring and full of opportunity.

KindleCo exists to help Public Sector & Social Purpose organisations meet these challenges to build perspective, create strategy, grow capability, to galvanise action.

Jo specialises in enabling organisations to find ways to turn external change into a driver for their own strategic development, working with four enduring principles: -

Collaboration, Compassion, Courage & Creativity

I'd like to invite you on a journey ....to nurture the meaning, resilience & impact in your work which will strengthen the future outcomes for your beneficiaries and your contribution to systems-change ...

I believe we all have the opportunity to make a positive impact on the lifechances of Children & Young People whether it is through cultivating stakeholders, brokering collaboration, galvanising political will or and unlocking investment (influence is not always counted in money).

With skills honed over more than 25 years, I facilitate, support & enable those working in a Government - influenced business environment, to develop themselves strategically. 

I aim to build self-efficacy & capability at organisational, team & individual level through:

Policy response, insight & shaping 

Strategy creation 

Brokerage 

Systems thinking, planning & expression

Evaluation

I share a passion for the stakeholder dynamics in key Policy areas -

Social Inclusion, Lifelong Learning (Education & Skills), Mental Health & Wellbeing, Creativity & the Arts

Expertise

Through KindleCo, I share experience gained over the past 25+ years of working in, with and through the Public Sector

I understand the challenges and the joys of the national landscape and of inter-agency working,  funding criteria and complex stakeholder dynamics at local, regional & sector levels.

If you enjoy a little bit of professional history, read on.....otherwise you can jump straight to my 2018 Services Flyer here

I originally trained as a Musician, and have spent many years directly coaching children, young people (and some adult learners ) in schools, colleges, in extended services settings and privately, building their capacity & capability for public performance. 

I furthered my passion for team work with a BMus. Hons in Conducting which I appreciate, can seem an odd qualification for 'strategy & organisational development'. 

But those disciplines are very closely aligned - getting the best out of people and creating something which is bigger than the sum of its individual parts. I entered the realms of working alongside the Public Sector in a strategic capacity in 1998 and in one way or another, have not left since. I think its a great place with committed professionals, along with those in the Voluntary, Charity and Social Enterprise sectors who partner them. If you would like a gander through an extended Biog, you can download one here.

I now work mostly through KindleCo as a single Consultant or Project Lead but small teams of associates can be arranged where needed.

What I don't do: I am not a communications, PR or marketing agency. But I will work with you to align these functions, within your overall organisational, partnership, project or personal strategy.

I welcome informal and exploratory chats. You can also Tweet me!
