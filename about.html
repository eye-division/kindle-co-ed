"we are living in times of Revolutionary change....for which there are no precedents...our children are confronting challenges that are unique in human history"
Sir Ken Robinson

Through KindleCo, I strive to build the capacity & capability of single organisations, strategic partnership, mixed-disciplinary projects, and their associated professionals through support for: 

  Organisational development - strategy creation & delivery

  Policy response - knowledge of the external landscape, insight & shaping 

  Project Management - robust oversight & implementation

  Resource development - brokerage, positioning & execution

  Systems thinking - analysis , design & expression in a landscape of change

 

I share experience built over more than 25 years of work with young people and in brokering, developing and designing multi-organisation programmes which bring mixed- disciplinary and cross-agency teams together to influence the agenda of decision -makers.
 
My values are to work with the four enduring principles of

Collaboration    Compassion    Courage    Creativity

I strive to bring knowledge, ideas, insights and practical solutions to the table which help make our work together policy-informed & practice-sensitive

I am at my most professionally passionate when working in key Public Policy areas of              Social Inclusion, Learning, Mental Health, the Arts

 

This site is a simple online portfolio where you will find a pick & mix of project profiles, client case studies and professional history which seek to reflect this approach and those values.

I am passionate about the value of collaboration in Social Purpose work, a committed advocate for equality of opportunity, lover of systems-change thinking, a believer in the transformative power of creativity

Expertise

I'd like to invite you on a journey ....to nurture the meaning, resilience & impact in your work, to strengthen outcomes for your end- beneficiaries and your contribution to the sector...Through KindleCo, I share experience gained over more than 25 years of working with children, young people and with the publicly-funded organisations who champion them

I have worked directly with children, young people and families and on their behalf, through public sector project design and the development of strategic partnerships at agency level. Now through Consultancy, Coaching and Advice Surgeries, I facilitate & enable Organisations to better understand the landscape external to their projects, to develop their workstream portfolios strategically and collaborate with meaning.

A little bit about Jo ....

My commitment to developing the skills, talents & lifechances of Children & Young People is longstanding, along with my belief in advocacy as an agent of change.

I believe success depends on getting the best out of people... and letting them get the best out of you...I originally trained as a Music Practitioner, spending many years as a group music coach and rehearsing Orchestras ready for performance. In that discipline, cultivating shared endeavour,  design-thinking is the essence of the work.

 

Read Jo's professional  history

In more recent years, and following a decade or so working in and with Cultural Services, I have honed my skills in work which develops projects, provision, or infrastructure around Families, Mental Health, SEND, Creativity and the Arts. I love to work with clients who have an ambition to develop their sector role in growing lifechances of young people. And I am a fan of advocacy, which has led me to Coaching and an interest in the role of Consultancy within Organisations. In 2016 I gained my first accreditation from the EMCC in Supervision of Consultants, Coaches and Mentors in the workplace.

For a deeper dive into my qualifications & training history please visit my LinkedIn page

The move from practitioner to strategist in the Public Sector began for me back in the 1990s when I moved to a Local Authority on Merseyside to work a Family Learning team, promoting the lifechances of vulnerable families through skills-building. In one guise or another, I have not left since. Despite its difficult challenges, the Public Sector - and its network of partners in the Charitable, Social Enterprise and Voluntary sectors- remains a vibrant place to work with the most committed professionals you will ever meet

The way we do business and interact has radically altered but the dynamics of change, influencing, collaboration and of shared purpose are still built on the quality of Relationships. We have an opportunity every day to make a direct & positive impact on the lifechances of Children & Young People......

Through KindleCo I deliver mostly as a single Consultant or Project Lead to government-funded organisations and their key partners

Read more about KindleCo's work since its inception in 2004 including a large-scale regional contract focused on programme re-design and development of sector partnerships; single locality and sub-regional consortia projects; public report-writing. I have established some long term consultancy relationships including with a national Charity for Vulnerable Families. Coaching is offered specific to public sector and related professionals, especially those taking on a new multi-agency role, Partnerships remit, or who are undergoing a workplace transition.
