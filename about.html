Do you want to turn unexpected change from feeling like your 'uninvited guest' into your best friend ? 

I will work with you collaboratively to help unlock your own capacity to create & embrace change, enabling you to tackle the challenges of transitions & unexpected events with purpose, authenticity and meaning

 
With a wealth of experience working with Children, Young People, Parent-Carers and Organisations as a Coach, Advisor and Mentor, I bring extensive professional experience to the table.
A former Programme & Partrnerships lead in the Children & Families Sector who has worked on local, regional and national programmes funded by Government, I now support Charities and Social Purpose projects working at the intersection of public policy, through my Consultancy. 
 

I have significant experience in my professional life of leading strategic 'change-maker' projects, of brokering partnerships and of supporting children's champions, so that diverse interests can be brought together to create impact, at moments of sector change.

As a personal Coach, I share my own experience of navigating unexpected change, at home, at work and everywhere in between! 

I hold an LTCL in Education, am a certified Life Coach and have undertaken EMCC-accredited certification in Supervision of Coaches, Mentors and Consultants. I am DBS checked and trained to Level 3 (Advanced) in Safeguarding. I am also a committed Trustee appointed to the Board of a Young Person's Charity.
Consultancy: helping organisations in the Children & Families sector, to navigate change
Mentoring: to private individuals & professional teams
Coaching 1:1: individual clients, helping to plot a course through life's unexpected events
