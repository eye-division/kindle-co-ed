Collaboration  Compassion  Courage  Creativity

 
KindleCo is a trusted partner - "supporting the supporters" - of Children, Young People & Families, since 2004
 
I bring more than 25 years of experience to the table of navigating the systems, strategies, agencies and public policy surrounding Children & - Young People, gained through settings including:
- Local Government & Family Learning
- Regional public sector agencies & the Arts
- Third Sector organisations, Education and Vulnerable children
- Government - funded national agencies, Learning & Skills
- Funding bodies
- Private Clients including individual professionals & parents-carers
 
 I deploy first-hand experience of:

Coaching children, young people and their supporting Adults 

Leading development & delivery of multi-stakeholder projects 

Navigating cross-boundary projects in the public sector

Supporting charities with development tasks

Enabling  practitioners & professionals through Coaching & Mentoring 

I specialise in partnering those delivering against a backdrop of public policy & systems 

Your commission does not have to be big for us to work together!
