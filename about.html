"we are living in times of Revolutionary change....for which there are no precedents...our children are confronting challenges that are unique in human history" (Sir Ken Robinson, Out of Our Minds, 2018)

Established in 2004 by Jo Broad, KindleCo is a specialist  independent service 'supporting the supporters' of Children, Young People & Families to thrive in a landscape of change ....

This site acts as a simple on-line portfolio for my professional work. I welcome informal approaches and am happy to discuss your project, professional or organisational needs in a no-obligation exploratory call. If you'd like to learn a little more about me and the work we might do together, read on...

Alternatively, skip to My Services or download here my Services Flyer

PURPOSE

I am committed to the value of collaboration in Social Purpose work, an advocate for equality of opportunity, believer in compassion-led design, lover of systems-change thinking and witness to the transformative power of creativity

Through KindleCo, I strive to share experience built over more than 25 years of work with young people, families and, the organisations that support them. I have led the design & development of strategic programmes and cross-boundary projects in the public sector, supported a range of private clients from education charities to public agencies, and enabled individual projects & professionals to reach their goals

My work is impact-driven and values-led 

"Collaboration   Compassion    Courage    Creativity"

I specialise in partnering those delivering against a backdrop of public policy

 who are driven by social purpose and on a mission for change

Change is not all about scale. It's about impact.

Your project does not have to be big for us to work together!

STORY

A little more about Jo .... my commitment to developing the lifechances of Children & Young People is longstanding

So is my belief in collaboration and systems-thinking, as agents of change

After a period working through the Cultural Services & Creative Industries on multi-agency projects at local and regional - agency levels, I have expanded my practice skills in more recent years to be able to support organisations delivering mainstream programmes for Wellbeing, Learning and Vulnerable children

I am a qualified Mental Health First-Aider (adult) with a passion for Workforce Development

A committed contributor to the principals of Co Production - long before it became fashionable - I have worked in local government in Family Learning serving some of the most deprived and vulnerable families of the UK.  Whether singly, or in strategic roles driving regional partnerships, I have worked with a multitude LAs both in diverse locations spanning Merseyside, Greater Manchester, London and East England

I have led strategic intervention work with public agencies including a regional portfolio with Arts Council England, leading the regional roll-out of national programmes.  Brokering cross-boundary partnerships between public sector, community-led organisations and private sector organisations gives me a sound grasp of corporate social responsibility and the dynamics of mixed-disciplinary group work.

IMPACT

Successfully influencing decision-makers to create provision and resources to benefit Children & Young People, through collaboration with others, is part of my track record.  I firmly believe 'Collaboration' is a value and a behaviour we choose to adopt, not just a process exclusively reserved for those operating at scale

I also understand the need for sustainability to remain centre-stage in Organisational Development and in sector-level change

I am able to share with clients my experience of winning bids for KindleCo contracts through competitive tendering, of leading successful funding applications to regional programmes on behalf of public agency partnerships, of building successful submissions to Government and European funding pots, such as the Standards Fund, Lottery and delegated ERDF

Although I do not undertake bid-writing as a stand-alone task, you can be sure I bring to the table experience which enables me to provide well-informed support to clients, in an age where future-proofing is key. I will help you ensure your strategic plans take care of the resources, language and strategic partnerships which can promote your sustainability

I now also Coach professionals working in and alongside the Public Sector in Education and other social purpose settings, to enable successful workplace transitions. I love supporting #carers

I undertake a small amount of Social Responsibility activity each year. I am currently an active member of the National Children's Bureau Family Research Advisory Group

 

You can learn more about the outcomes of my work by visiting       Case Studies

 

Working Together

What will we have in common?

A passion, need or commitment to delivering against a canvass of public policy, in a mixed economy of providers, under diverse political leadership, for end-beneficiaries of Children, Young People & Families

What work could we do together ?  In a nutshell.... 

  • deepen an understanding of your external environment, navigate and shape it through policy-response & strategy development
  • build meaningful alliances around your purpose through stakeholder engagement and strategic partnerships 
  • deploy external change as a driver for your own project or organisational development

What would the outcomes be?

Every piece of work & every community is different. All my consultancy is bespoke. But alongside specific task objectives, I strive to build:

  • Knowledge, Confidence, Capacity & Capability
  • A rounded 360 view of resource development: this is a "must" in today's fiscal climate,  so expect our discussions to include developing objectives around strategic partnerships, sector positioning, and stakeholders, as well as funding
