Through KindleCo, I share a commitment to equality of opportunity and a passion for sector dynamics in the key Public Policy areas of Social Inclusion, Lifelong Learning (Education & Skills), Mental Health, the Arts and Creativity.

I strive to enable the organisations & individuals who keep the Children & Families sector afloat, to turn external change into a driver for their own strategic development.

Working with my four enduring principles of Collaboration, Compassion, Courage & Creativity

KindleCo exists to enable our sector champions to take forward their ambitions:

 to build perspective, create pragmatic strategies, grow capability and galvanise action through the development of stakeholder-rich projects, multi-agency partnerships, organisations and the professionals who drive them.

"we are living in times of Revolutionary change....for which there are no precedents...our children are confronting challenges that are unique in human history"

(Sir Ken Robinson, Out of Our Minds, 3rd Edition, 2017)
 

For the Public Sector & its Social Purpose partners, the job of looking ahead, understanding the external landscape, responding and moving forward, has never been more challenging

The nature, pace and scope of current societal change is eye-watering. In stakeholder-rich environments influenced by public policy, it can feel overwhelming, and inspiring.

It is also full of opportunity.

With skills honed over more than 25 years, I facilitate, support & enable those working in a Government - influenced business environment, to develop themselves strategically, find opportunity in change and in working with others. 

At organisation, team, stakeholder & individual professional levels, I strive to build self-efficacy & capability in others through support for:

  Policy - understanding, response, insight & shaping 

  Strategy - creation, interpretation & execution

  Brokerage - planning, positioning, enacting

  Systems - thinking & expression

  Evaluation - reflecting, knowing, changing

I'd like to invite you on a journey ....to nurture the meaning, resilience & impact in your work, to strengthen outcomes for your end- beneficiaries and your ongoing contribution to positive systems-change ...

Expertise

Through KindleCo, I share experience gained over the more than 25 years of working in, with & through publicly - funded services, organisations, projects and their extended network of business partners

If you enjoy a bit of professional history, you can read more of mine here ....or you can jump straight to my 2018 Services Flyer 

I originally trained as a Musician and grew up in the English state school & Music Conservatoire systems. I found these to be a 'place for all', socially -speaking. 

Professionally, I spent many years directly coaching children, young people (and some adults) in schools, colleges, in extended services settings and privately, building their capacity & capability for public performance. 

I furthered my passion for team work with a degree in Conducting (yes the baton-waving type, only I never used one of those...) and I do recognise, this can seem an odd qualification for a later career in Public Sector projects and organisational development....

But those disciplines are very closely aligned - success depends on getting the best out of people, cultivating shared endeavour, building structure behind the scenes and most of all, creating something collaboratively far greater, than the sum of the individual parts.....

Between training, personal and professional gigs I have performed in the UK (London, Edinburgh, Glasgow, Bath) and Europe (Germany, Italy, Poland, Romania) with groups ranging from age 8 to 80. I don't regularly practice in the field now but have retained my contact with group professional practice and a commitment to nurturing the lifechances of Children & Young People through project, partnerships and O.D.

People who know me well, say I am at my happiest and most effective, in groups. I think they are probably right.

Here's one chosen at random, taken with the fabulous One Stage Concert Tours, an amazing women-led enterprise which has helped give positive life experiences to young people in partnership with Local Authorities, and a few adults! 

Working alongside the Public Sector in a strategic capacity, and as part of a Partnership effort, began for me in earnest in 1998 and in one guise or another, I have not left since. Despite its difficult challenges - including all the clammer about funding levels - the Public Sector remains a vibrant place to work, with the most committed professionals you will ever meet.

You have the privileged opportunity every day, of making a direct & positive impact on the lifechances of Children & Young People.

Through KindleCo, I now deliver mostly as a single Consultant or Project Lead to government-funded organisations and their key partners. In the right circumstances, teams of associates have been made possible in the past for larger-scale commissions.

 

What I don't do: I am not a communications, PR or marketing agency. But I will work with you to align these functions, within your overall organisational, partnership, project or personal strategy.

I welcome informal and exploratory chats. You can also Tweet me!

I'm a recovering social media introvert @jobroad_kin
