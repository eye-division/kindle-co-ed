"we may not be able to predict the future, but we can help shape it" (Sir Ken Robinson) 

 

"Collaboration  Compassion  Courage  Creativity"

 
Since 2004, KindleCo has been a trusted, specialist, independent partner 'supporting the supporters' of Children, Young People & Families to thrive in a landscape of change .... 
 
Using that experience, I partner organisations and individuals working with Social, Educational and Civic purpose across a range of intersecting policy areas
to build their own capacity 
whilst they capacity-build others

Short on time? Skip straight to My Services or download here my Services Flyer

I share experience built over more than 25 years of work with young people, families and the organisations that support them. I have led the design & development of strategic programmes and cross-boundary projects in the public sector, supported a range of private clients from education charities to public agencies, and enabled individual projects & professionals to reach their goals 

I specialise in partnering those delivering against a backdrop of public policy

Change is about impact

but your commission does not have to be big for us to work together!

Whilst I do take on large projects, I  also offer one-off Surgeries as a confidential space for organisations to discuss ongoing development or project set up & exit challenges

I also work with individual professionals and with parent-carers

Coaching and Mentoring can be made accessible as a taster session before any larger commitment is made
