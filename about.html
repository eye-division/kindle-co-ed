those working in Government - influenced business environment

change & collaborate

I originate professionally from the Music industry and have retained my contact with 'group practice' through Organisations, along with a firm commitment to nurturing the lifechances of Children & Young People.

Focussed now on work which develops provision for Vulnerable Families including Mental Health,  SEND, Creativity & the Arts, I work with clients who have an ambition to 'develop skills' in young people and their supporters, across a broad spectrum of settings

For a deeper dive into my qualifications & training history please visit my LinkedIn page

Working alongside the Public Sector in a strategic capacity began for me back in the 1990s in the Cultural Sector and in one guise or another, I have not left since.

Despite its difficult challenges, the Public Sector - and its network of partners in the Charitable, Social Enterprise and Voluntary sectors- remains a vibrant place to work with the most committed professionals you will ever meet.

And whilst the way we do business and interact has radically altered in that time, the dynamics of change, influence and shared purpose have not: they are still built on the quality of Relationships

 

We have an opportunity every day to make a direct & positive impact on the lifechances of    Children & Young People

- Consultancy relationships over the longterm:
