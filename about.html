Consultancy

With a wealth of experience working with Children, Young People, Parent-Carers and Organisations as a Organisational Consultant, Coach and Mentor
I bring extensive professional experience to the table.
I share my skills & experience of navigating sector change to help organisations stay rooted in their purpose and create their own meaningful shifts so they work with, rather than swim against, the powerful pull of sector change. 

 

A former Programme & Partrnerships lead in the Children & Families Sector who has worked on local, regional and national programmes funded by Government.
As an organisaional Consultant, I now support Charities and Social Purpose projects working at the intersection of public policy, to stay alinged with values and mission, in a landscape of change to create tangible outcomes.  I draw on significant experience in my professional life of leading strategic 'change-maker' projects, of brokering partnerships and of supporting children's champions, so that diverse interests can be brought together to create impact, at moments of sector change.

 

I hold an LTCL in Education and have undertaken EMCC-accredited certification in Supervision of Coaches, Mentors and Consultants. I am DBS checked (enhamced, adult & child) and trained to Level 3 (Advanced) in Safeguarding. I have previous expereince as a Trustee on the Board of a Young Person's Charity.
