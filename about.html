"we are living in times of Revolutionary change....for which there are no precedents...our children are confronting challenges that are unique in human history"
Sir Ken Robinson

Established in 2004, KindleCo is a specialist and  independent service whose mission is to 'support the supporters' of Children, Young People & Families in a landscape of change

KindleCo is impact-driven and values-led 

My values are to work with the four enduring principles of:

Collaboration    Compassion    Courage    Creativity

I strive to build knowledge, capacity & capability in organisations, their strategic partnerships, stakeholder networks and in professionals delivering in the context of public policy, through support for:

  Organisational development - strategy creation & implementation

  Policy response - knowing the external landscape, understanding, growing & shaping 

  Project Planning & Management - of multi-party initiatives, their oversight & delivery 

  Partnerships & Resource development - brokerage, positioning, funding & collaborations

  Systems thinking - analysis , design & contribution 

I share experience built over more than 25 years of work with young people themselves and in the brokerage, development and design of multi-organisation programmes. 
 
For a deeper dive into my qualifications & training please visit my LinkedIn page.
 

I strive to bring knowledge, ideas, insights and practical solutions to the table which help make our work together policy-informed & practice-sensitive

I am at my most professionally passionate when working in key Public Policy areas of              Social Inclusion    Learning    Mental Health  the Arts

I am committed to the value of collaboration in Social Purpose work, an advocate for equality of opportunity, a lover of systems-change thinking and a believer in the transformative power of creativity

On this site you will find a pick & mix of project profiles, client case studies and professional history which seek to reflect this approach and those values.

Expertise

I have worked directly with children, young people & families and with the public sector in project development and strategic partnerships for over 25 years.  Now I deliver my contribution to the sector through Consultancy, Coaching, Project Advice Surgeries and Project Management.  

 

A little more about Jo ....

My commitment to developing the skills, talents & lifechances of Children & Young People is longstanding, along with my belief in advocacy as an agent of change. 
 
Following a decade or so working on national scale projects at local and regional agency level, including through Cultural Services and the Creative Industries, I have focused my attention in more recent years exclusively on work which supports organisations delivering in complex stakeholder environments for the benefit of Families. Learning, Mental Health and SEND have become a focus whilst Creativity and the Arts remain close to my heart. 
 

I hold a Certificate from EMCC in Supervision of Consultants, Coaches and Mentors in the workplace.

I originally trained as a Music Practitioner.  It sounds an odd connection to what I do now, granted, but actually those disciplines have much in common - team work, coaching for performance, knowing the internal and external environment. Creating a 'bigger picture' greater than the sum of the parts.
 
Orchestras are communities where hard work, skill, compassion, respect and diversity are the lifeblood. Collaboration matters.
 
The 1990s brought a move to a Local Authority on Merseyside to work in a Family Learning team, promoting the lifechances of our most vulnerable families.  Despite its difficult challenges, the Public Sector, and its network of partners in the Charitable, Social Enterprise and Voluntary sectors, remains a vibrant place with the most committed professionals you will ever meet. The way we do business and interact has radically altered but the dynamics of change, influencing, collaboration and of shared purpose are still built on the quality of Relationships. 
 
We have an opportunity every day to make a direct & positive impact on the lifechances of Children & Young People......
