Consultancy

I share my skills & experience of navigating the public sector to help organisations stay rooted in their purpose and create their own meaningful shifts by working with, rather than against, the powerful pull of sector change.

With a wealth of experience working with Children, Young People, Parent-Carers and with Organisations

I bring deep & rounded professional & lived experience to the table.

 Prior to establishing KindleCo, I led a wide range of intervention programmes in the Children & Families sector, in local government, with regional agencies and working in national programmes funded by Government.

As an organisational Consultant, KindleCo supports Charities and Social Purpose projects working at the intersection of public policy, to stay aligned with values and mission, in a landscape of change to create tangible next steps. I work with leaders to enable diverse internal and external concerns to be brought together, at moments of sector change.

 

I hold an LTCL in Education and have undertaken EMCC-accredited certification in Supervision of Coaches, Mentors and Consultants. I am DBS checked (enhanced, adult & child) and trained to Level 3 (Advanced) in Safeguarding. I have previous experience as a Trustee on the Board of a Young Person's Charity.
