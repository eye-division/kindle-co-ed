Sir Ken Robinson Out of Our Minds 2017
 
For the Public Sector & its Social Purpose partners the job of looking ahead, understanding the external landscape, responding and moving forward, has never been more challenging...
 
It can feel overwhelming....but it's also inspiring and full of opportunity
 

I strive to build capacity & capability in organisations, p artnerships, sectors & individuals 

Through: 

 Policy - understanding, response, insight & shaping 

  Strategy - creation, interpretation & delivery

  Brokerage - planning, positioning, execution

  Systems - thinking & expression

  Evaluation - knowing & evolving in a landscape of change

 

KindleCo allows me to share experience built over 25 years 

Having worked directly with children, young people and families and on their behalf through public sector project design, strategic partnerships development and management of multi-agency programmes, I now share that experience with professionals working in Government - influenced business environments.

I facilitate, support & enable Project, Team and Organisational Leaders to understand their external landscape, develop their portfolios strategically, collaborate & find opportunity in change.

 

I am passionate about the value of collaboration in Social Purpose work, a committed advocate for equality of opportunity, lover of systems-change thinking, a believer in the transformative power of creativity.

 

On this site you will find a pick & mix of project profiles, client case studies, professional history and a smattering of inspiration from others which in one way or another, reflect those values

KindleCo specialises in supporting sector champions in the key Public Policy areas of Social Inclusion, Lifelong Learning (Education & Skills), Mental Health and the Arts

 

Working with my four enduring principles 

Collaboration, Compassion, Courage & Creativity 

 

KindleCo exists to enable our sector champions to take forward their ambitions growing perspective, creating strategy, nurturing capacity and galvanising action

My clients are likely to be delivering settings which are mixed-disciplinary or multi-agency, be seeking outcomes which help build infrastructure in their sector, organisation, team or in the individuals who work to grow the lifechances of Children, Young People & Families

I'd like to invite you on a journey ....to nurture the meaning, resilience & impact in your work, to strengthen outcomes for your end- beneficiaries and your contribution to the sector...

A little bit about Jo ....

My commitment to developing the skills, talents & lifechances of Children & Young People is longstanding, along with my belief in advocacy as an agent of change.

Read Jo's professional  history

Success depends on getting the best out of people, cultivating shared endeavour, design-thinking, building structure behind the scenes and most of all, creating an something collaboratively where the outcome is far greater than the sum of the individual parts.....

Picture featured by kind permission of OneStage.co.uk

I originate professionally from the Music sector - a group music coach. I have retained my contact with 'group practice' until the present day through Organisations, along with a firm commitment to nurturing the lifechances of Children & Young People

Focussed now on work which develops provision for Vulnerable Families including Mental Health,  SEND, Creativity & the Arts, I work with clients who have an ambition to develop skills in young people and their supporters, across a broad spectrum of settings

For a deeper dive into my qualifications & training history please visit my LinkedIn page

Working alongside the Public Sector in a strategic capacity began for me back in the 1990s in the Cultural Sector and in one guise or another, I have not left since

Despite its difficult challenges, the Public Sector - and its network of partners in the Charitable, Social Enterprise and Voluntary sectors- remains a vibrant place to work with the most committed professionals you will ever meet

And whilst the way we do business and interact has radically altered in that time, the dynamics of change, influence and shared purpose have not: they are still built on the quality of Relationships

 

We have an opportunity every day to make a direct & positive impact on the lifechances of Children & Young People

Through KindleCo I deliver mostly as a single Consultant or Project Lead to government-funded organisations and their key partners

Since 2004, commissions have included large-scale regional briefs, including one to model the spin-out of a Government-run initiative; development of sector partnerships in a single locality and across a  sub-regional in Consortia; the preparation of editorial for peer-reviewed articles; public report-writing; consultancy relationships over the longterm including a national Charity at the cutting edge of innovation in the support of Vulnerable Families

I also now undertake individual Coaching, working with professionals undergoing a working life transition.
