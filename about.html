Through KindleCo, I share experience built over 25 years in public sector project design, development of strategic partnerships, nurture of stakeholder initiatives and  management of multi-agency programmes.

I am passionate about the value of collaboration in Social Purpose work, a committed advocate for equality of opportunity, lover of systems-change thinking and believe in the transformative power of creativity.

KindleCo specialises in supporting our sector champions in the key Public Policy areas of Social Inclusion, Lifelong Learning (Education & Skills), Mental Health and the Arts in their endeavours, specifically - 

Organisational development, the growth of collaborative Partnerships, the shaping of Projects as strategic interventions and leading Programme roll out.

I strive to enable the organisations & individuals who keep the Children & Families sector afloat, to turn external change, into a driver for their own strategic development.

Working with my four enduring principles of 

Collaboration, Compassion, Courage & Creativity 

KindleCo exists to enable our sector champions to take forward their ambitions:

 by growing perspective, creating strategy, nurturing capacity and galvanising action

Your work is likely to be delivered in a setting which is mixed-disciplinary or multi-agency in nature and seeks outcomes which help build infrastructure in your sector, organisation, team or individuals.

"we are living in times of Revolutionary change....for which there are no precedents...our children are confronting challenges that are unique in human history"

(Sir Ken Robinson, Out of Our Minds, 3rd Edition, 2017)
 

For the Public Sector & its Social Purpose partners, the job of looking ahead, understanding the external landscape, responding and moving forward, has never been more challenging

The nature, pace and scope of current societal change is eye-watering. In environments driven by public policy, it can feel overwhelming. 

It can also be inspiring and full of opportunity.

With skills honed over more than 25 years, I facilitate, support & enable those working in a Government - influenced business environment, to develop themselves strategically, find opportunity in change and in collaborating with others. 

I strive to build agency, self-efficacy & capability in others, through support for:

  Policy - understanding, response, insight & shaping 

  Strategy - creation, interpretation & delivery

  Brokerage - planning, positioning, execution

  Systems - thinking & expression

  Evaluation - consulting & change

I'd like to invite you on a journey ....to nurture the meaning, resilience & impact in your work, to strengthen outcomes for your end- beneficiaries and your contribution to the sector...

Expertise

Through KindleCo, I share experience gained over the more than 25 years of working in, with & through publicly - funded services, organisations, projects and their extended network of business partners

If you enjoy a bit of professional history, you can read more of mine here ....or you can jump straight to my 2018 Services Flyer 

I originally trained as a Musician and grew up in the English state school & Music Conservatoire systems. I found these to be a 'place for all', socially -speaking. 

Professionally, I spent many years directly coaching children, young people (and some adults) in schools, colleges, in extended services settings and privately, building their capacity & capability for public performance. 

I furthered my passion for team work with a degree in Conducting (yes the baton-waving type, only I never used one of those...) with a dollop of Research. I do recognise, this can seem an odd qualification for a later career in developing Public Sector projects, Sector Infrastructure and Organisations.

But those disciplines are very closely aligned - success depends on getting the best out of people, cultivating shared endeavour, building structure behind the scenes and most of all, creating something collaboratively far greater, than the sum of the individual parts.....

Whilst I don't regularly practice in the music field now, I  have retained my contact with group professional practice and a commitment to nurturing the lifechances of Children & Young People through work intersecting the policy areas which impact them, notably Learning, Mental Health, Vulnerable Families including SEMH, the Arts and Creative Industries.

 

Working alongside the Public Sector in a strategic capacity, and as part of a Partnership effort, began for me in earnest in 1998 and in one guise or another, I have not left since. Despite its difficult challenges - including all the clammer about funding levels - the Public Sector remains a vibrant place to work, with the most committed professionals you will ever meet.

 

You have the privileged opportunity every day, of making a direct & positive impact on the lifechances of Children & Young People.

Through KindleCo, I now deliver mostly as a single Consultant or Project Lead to government-funded organisations and their key partners. Teams of associates have been deployed in the past for larger-scale commissions.

 

What I don't do: I am not a communications, PR or marketing agency. But I will work with you to align these functions, within your overall organisational, partnership, project or personal strategy.

I welcome informal and exploratory chats. You can also Tweet me!
