Since 2004, KindleCo has been a trusted, specialist, independent partner 'supporting the supporters' of Children, Young People & Families to thrive in a landscape of change .... 
 
Using that experience, I partner organisations and individuals working with Social, Educational and Civic purpose across a range of intersecting policy areas
to build their own capacity 
whilst they capacity-build others

This site acts as a simple on-line portfolio for my professional work. I'm happy to discuss your project, individual profession or organisational needs in a no-obligation exploratory call.

PURPOSE

If your work is impact-driven and values-led, good news! 
We already have something in common
 

Here are some things which will bind us in shared purpose, should we work together:

 recognising the special value of Collaboration in social and educational purpose work

 believing in equality of opportunity 

 a love of systems - thinking

 believing compassion - led design of services is possible

If you are open and curious about such things, or already have your own way of showing commitment to them, we will likely work extremely well together.

Working Together

What will we have in common?

A passion and / or need to delivering in an environment which intersects public policy areas,  under diverse political leadership, for end-beneficiaries of

Children, Young People, Young Adults, their Families, their Champions

Communities - of interest, of practice, of place

What work could we do together as an organisation ?  

deepen an understanding of your external environment, navigate and shape it

contextualise your upcoming work streams and projects to align with external sector factors, to help make them more sustainable 

identify meaningful alliances of practical operational value, built around your purpose, including stakeholder engagement and strategic partnerships 

deploy external change as a driver for your own project or organisational development

What could the outcomes be?

Every piece of work & every community is different. All my consultancy is bespoke. Alongside specific task objectives, I strive to build: 

Knowledge, Confidence, Capacity & Capability

A rounded 360 view of resource development: this is a "must" in today's fiscal climate,  so expect our discussions to include developing objectives around strategic partnerships, sector positioning, and stakeholders, as well as funding

What work could we do together as an individual?  

 work through together your current and upcoming challenges, be those to your own career, wellbeing, social health or support in advocating for your child
