Collaboration  Compassion  Courage  Creativity

Since 2004, KindleCo has been delivering purpose-driven work as a trusted partner 'supporting the supporters' of Children, Young People & Families

 
I bring 25+ years experience to the table of navigating the systems, strategies and public policy surrounding Children & Young People,
 
I deploy first-hand experience of coaching Children, Young People and their supporting Adults 
leading 'change-maker' projects with the agencies that support them
 
My aim is always  to build on your strengths to build your capacity & resilience, so you can get on with the job, of  supporting others
 

I have led the design & development of strategic programmes and cross-boundary projects in the public sector, supported a range of private clients from education charities to public agencies.

I also enable individual practitioners & professionals to reach their goals through Coaching & Mentoring 

I specialise in partnering those delivering against a backdrop of public policy & systems 

 

Your commission does not have to be big for us to work together!

Whilst I do take on large projects, I  also offer one-off Surgeries as a confidential space for organisations to discuss ongoing development or project set up & exit challenges

I also work with individual parent-carers & advocates

Coaching and Mentoring can be made accessible as a taster session before any larger commitment is made
