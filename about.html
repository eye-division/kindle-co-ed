Do you want to turn unexpected change from feeling like your 'uninvited guest' into your best friend ? 

I will work with you collaboratively to help unlock your own capacity to create & embrace change, enabling you to tackle the challenges of transitions & unexpected events with purpose, authenticity and meaning

 
With a wealth of experience working with Children, Young People, Parent-Carers and Organisations as a Coach, Advisor and Mentor,
I bring extensive professional experience to the table.
As a personal Coach, I share my skills & experience of navigating unexpected change, at home, at work and everywhere in between, to help clients create meaning from change and re-imagine purposeful futures. 
 
A former Programme & Partrnerships lead in the Children & Families Sector who has worked on local, regional and national programmes funded by Government.
As an organisaional Consultant, I now support Charities and Social Purpose projects working at the intersection of public policy, to stay alinged with values and mission, in a landscape of change to create tangible outcomes. 
 

I draw on significant experience in my professional life of leading strategic 'change-maker' projects, of brokering partnerships and of supporting children's champions, so that diverse interests can be brought together to create impact, at moments of sector change.

I hold an LTCL in Education, am a certified Life Coach and have undertaken EMCC-accredited certification in Supervision of Coaches, Mentors and Consultants. I am DBS checked and trained to Level 3 (Advanced) in Safeguarding. I am also a committed Trustee appointed to the Board of a Young Person's Charity.
