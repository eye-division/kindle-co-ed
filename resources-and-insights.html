What makes working with me, Jo Broad, unique?

I am expert in supporting individuals & organisations working with a backdrop of public policy & government systems around children, young people & families. So, at work, I have navigated landscapes of continual change. I have personal experience, too,  of forced- change and career endings due to external circumstances. This means I don't just 'talk the talk' on adjusting to personal & professional change, but have 'walked the walk' too!
 

This all enables me to 'walk in your shoes'. 

 

What work could we do together 1:1 through Coaching ?

Compassionately examine your current challenges in life, at home or at work, and the role 'change' is playing in these. We will dial back in to your sense of purpose, innate talents & abilities to enable you to generate a structured plan for next steps and lead yourself confidently into new horizons.
 

What work could we do together as an organisation ?  

As a Consultant,  I can help you identify opportunities arising from change 

Scenario-plan & align upcoming work streams, projects or development plans with external sector landscape, both current & anticipated, in times of flux

Relationship build: re-focus attention on shared purpose, including with stakeholders 

What work could we do together through Mentoring ?

Support project teams as times of change, to stay aligned with values and mission.
Support individuals, as they navigate change due to external factors and circumstances, to draw on their skills & talents and find work-arounds to prevent obstacles becoming a dead-end.
 

What would the outcomes be ?

Every piece of work & every community is different. All my Consultancy & Coaching is tailored but in our work together you can expect to grow your Knowledge, Confidence, Capabilities, Self-Determination

What I don't do
For organisations: I am not a communications, PR or marketing agency. But I will work with you and your business partners to help align these functions, within your overall organisational, partnership or project strategy. 
For individuals: I am not a medical professional, therapist or councellor. But I will use my expert knowledge & expereince to Coach or Mentor you through a period of change, at work or at home, so that you can move on to your next steps with a greater sense of confidence and self-determination.
