Working Together

What work could we do together as an organisation ?  

deepen an understanding of your external environment, navigate and shape it

contextualise your upcoming work streams and projects to align with external sector factors, to help make them more sustainable 

identify meaningful alliances of practical operational value, built around your purpose, including stakeholder engagement and strategic partnerships 

deploy external change as a driver for your own project or OD

What could the outcomes be?

Every piece of work & every community is different. All my consultancy is bespoke but in all I do I strive to build: 

Knowledge & Confidence, Capacity & Capability

A rounded 360 view of resource development: this is a "must" in today's fiscal climate,  so expect our discussions to include developing objectives around strategic partnerships, sector positioning, and stakeholders, as well as funding

What work could we do together as an individual?  

 work through together your current and upcoming challenges, be those to your own career, wellbeing, social health or support in advocating for your child

What I don't do: I am not a communications, PR or marketing agency. But I will work with you and your business partners to help align these functions, within your overall organisational, partnership or project strategy
