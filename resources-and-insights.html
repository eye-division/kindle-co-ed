What work could we do together as an organisation ?  

Contextualise: upcoming work streams and projects to align with external sector factors, scenario-plan and aid future-proofing 

Identify: opportunities including for strategic alliances. Forge relationships of real practical operational value, built around shared purpose, including with stakeholders 

Deploy: external change as a driver for project or organisational development

Deliver: time-limited or intervention projects. Set up or exit of Services

What would the outcomes be?

Every piece of work & every community is different.

All my consultancy is bespoke. However, I strive to build in all I do: 

Knowledge, Confidence, Capacity & Capability

 

What work could we do together as an individual?  

 work through together your current and upcoming challenges, be those to your own career, wellbeing, social health or support in advocating for your child

What I don't do: I am not a communications, PR or marketing agency. But I will work with you and your business partners to help align these functions, within your overall organisational, partnership or project strategy
