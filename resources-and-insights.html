What makes KindleCo different to other development Consultancies & Coaches ?

My work is tailored to those working with a backdrop of public policy, government systems or purposeful enterprise 
 
policy - informed, practice - sensitive,  values-led
 

What work could we do together as an organisation ?  

Identify opportunities: including strategic alliances

Scenario-plan & align: upcoming work streams, projects or development plans with external sector landscape to aid future-proofing 

Relationship build: streamline those of real operational & strategic value built around shared purpose, including with stakeholders 

Deliver: time-limited & intervention projects, set up or exit services

What work could we do together 1:1 ?  

Address your current and upcoming challenges, be those to your own career & wellbeing or the support you give in advocating for a child or YP

What would the outcomes be ?

Every piece of work & every community is different. All my consultancy & coaching briefs are individual and bespoke. However I will strive to build in all I do your: 

Knowledge, Confidence, Capacity & Capability

Your commission does not have to be big for us to work together!I 

I also offer

One-off Surgeries

A confidential space for organisations to discuss ongoing development,  project set up & exit 

Coaching & Mentoring as a Taster Session 

What I don't do: I am not a communications, PR or marketing agency. But I will work with you and your business partners to help align these functions, within your overall organisational, partnership or project strategy. I'm not a clinician and I won't offer you any medical opinions. If I feel you or the children you are working with are unsafe, I will signpost you to an appropriate level of support.
