What makes working with me, Jo Broad, different from other, less specialised Consultancies?

I am expert in supporting individuals & organisations

working with a backdrop of public policy & government systems around children, young people & families.

 Through my 30 years in the sector, I have successfully navigated landscapes of continual change. This means I don't just 'talk the talk' on adjusting to sector change, but have 'walked the walk' too, at the front line! Through my Organisational Consultancy, I have helped 100s of professionals to do the same.

I strive to 'walk in your shoes'. 

 

What work could we do together as an organisation ?  

As a Consultant,  I can help you identify opportunities arising from change including: 

Scenario-plan & align 

upcoming work streams, projects or development plans with external sector landscape, both current & anticipated, in times of flux

Relationship building focussing attention on shared purpose, including with your stakeholders

Strategic shaping & planning sensitive to both staying true to your mission & values, whilst working with the opportunities afforded by sector change, rather than swimming against it 

What could the likely outcomes be ?

Every piece of work & every community is different. All my Consultancy & Coaching is tailored but in our work together you can expect to grow your and your staff's Knowledge, Confidence, Capabilities, Self-Determination in the face of sector change

What I don't do
For organisations: I am not a communications, PR or marketing agency. But I will work with you and your business partners to help align these functions, within your overall organisational, partnership or project strategy.
