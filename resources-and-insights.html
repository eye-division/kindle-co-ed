What makes KindleCo different?

My work is tailored to those working with a backdrop of public policy & government systems in support of children, young people & young adults
 
My expertise are:
policy - informed : practice - sensitive : values-led
 

What work could we do together as an organisation ?  

Identify opportunities: including strategic alliances

Scenario-plan & align: upcoming work streams, projects or development plans with external sector landscape to aid future-proofing 

Relationship build: streamline those of real operational & strategic value built around shared purpose, including with stakeholders 

Deliver: time-limited & intervention projects, set up or exit services

What work could we do together 1:1 ?  

Address your current and upcoming challenges, be those to your own career & wellbeing or the support you give in advocating for a child or YP

What would the outcomes be ?

Every piece of work & every community is different. All my consultancy & coaching is tailored. In our work together you can expect to grow your:

Knowledge, Confidence, Capacity, Capabilities

Your commission does not have to be big for us to work together
