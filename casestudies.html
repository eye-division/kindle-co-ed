An outstanding Consultant to both organisations and individuals supporting Children & Young People. She is a breath of fresh air...

Rob Barton, Trustee, Education Charity and Director of RBA Search, Oxfordshire 2019

Discreet, professional and positive, Jo’s input helps ensure we meet the challenges of sector change with confidence    

John Diamond, CEO, national children's charity, The Mulberry Bush Organisation 2018
