KindleCo's mission is to enable those working in the interests of children, young people & families, to thrive 

Established by me, Jo Broad, KindleCo has been a specialist business partner, coach & mentor to champions of Children, Young People & Families since 2004
 
I bring 25+ years experience to the table of navigating the systems, strategies and public policy surrounding Children & Young People. I deploy first-hand experience of coaching Children, Young People and Adults and of leading strategic 'change-maker' projects with the agencies that support them.
