KindleCo's mission is to enable those working with social, educational and civic purpose to thrive

As a specialist partner to champions of Children, Young People & Families since 2004, KindleCo enables Organisational, Programme, Partnership and Personal development for anyone working with systems, strategy and public policy around Children & Young People.
 
Established by me, Jo Broad , KindleCo provides specialist consultancy, project management and individual coaching
 
My clients are organisations, strategic partnerships, stand-alone projects projects, professionals and parent-carers
 
Over the past 17 years, I have enabled clients with tasks of all shapes and sizes to navigate a way forward, find their confidence and sustain their own support for their stakeholders and families. 
 
 

Four professional values, drive all my work: 

Compassion, Collaboration, Courage, Creativity 

 

Using over 25 years of first-hand experience of coaching Children, Young People & Young Adults, and leading 'change-maker' projects with the agencies that support them, I understand what drives this community.

I understand the challenges you face.
 
You may be the CEO of a small charity striving to reconcile the internal needs of your organisation with the bigger picture outside....

Or a Project Manager with a complex stakeholder relationships to align....

Or perhaps a parent-carer bamboozled by the system whilst trying to advocate for your child....

I will bring expert knowledge & insight to the table to aid you in opportunity - spotting and future - proofing for your organisation.

If you're a private individual, we will build your confidence & capability together in understanding the external landscape and navigating the system.

Whether as Consultant or Coach, I will bring compassion and challenge to our shared task, enabling you to find your own best way forward.

I offer:

 Consultancy - for organisations delivering at the nexus of public policy, government strategy & stakeholder networks

Programme leadership - for multi-stakeholder projects 

Project  Management - specialist partner & contractor 

  Surgeries - advice for project inception, transition & exit 

Coaching -  projects, partnerships, professionals, parent-carers

This site is intended as an online portfolio. It aspires to give you a flavour of my background, experience and the work of my clients & partners. It isn't a sales site. I am always happy to talk through your project ideas, sustainability challenges, strategy formation or stakeholder needs  for organisations - or your advocacy challenges for a young person - in a free 30 minute  consultation.

I look forward to supporting you as you raise ambitions, shape your goals and deliver with your purpose in heart and mind
