Hi, I'm Jo - a personal Coach, Mentor and organisational Consultant with 30 years experience of working through change in the Children & Families sector.

I specialise in helping clients get the best out of change & unexpected events.

My mission is very simple -  to enable my clients to overcome obstacles & thrive!

Life often throws change at us, at home, at work and from external forces. Sometimes we don't see it coming. But not all change is bad. In fact it can be a great catalyst for progress & growth, even when we are not expecting it. But it can feel overwhelming & unwelcome - like an 'uninvited guest' who turns up, sits in our favourite chair and then walks off in our best shoes!

I'm here to help you tame the beast - to turn change, from feeling like an uninvited guest, into your best friend.

My Consultancy practice: I specialise in partnering organisations who are champions of Children & Young People, enabling them to stay rooted in insightful strategy & partnerships, through the choppy waters of change.
 
My Coaching practice: helps individuals to deal with change, including the unexpected & uninvited, enabling clients to use it a catalyst for exploration of new horizons and futures.
 
I bring 30 years experience of creating 'moments of change' with organisations and individuals, espcially those supporting Children & Young People, to the table.
