Life often throws change at us, at home and at work. Like an 'uninvited guest' who turns up, sits in your favourite chair and then walks off in your best shoes!

I'm here to help you turn unexpected change, from being the squattor in your life, into being your superpower !

 

Hi, I'm Jo. I'm an organisational Consultant in the Children & Families sector with 30 years experience of working through change. I'm also a personal Coach & Mentor.

I specialise in helping clients get the best out of change & unexpected events.

My mission is very simple -  to enable my clients to overcome obstacles & thrive!

As my client, I will help and guide you to:

  • stay connected to your core values, innate talents, purpose & abilities
  • develop strategies which enable you to embrace uninvited change, so it becomes part of your roadmap, and not a dead end
  • nurture self-awareness & deal constructively with limiting beliefs
  • be ready to confidently step forward into a self-determined plan of action, with you holding your own reigns of change
My Consultancy practice: specialises in partnering organisational clients who are champions of Children & Young People, enabling them to stay rooted in insightful strategy & partnerships, through the choppy waters of change.
My private Coaching practice: helps individuals to deal with change, including the unexpected & uninvited, enabling clients to use it a catalyst for exploring new horizons.
I bring 30 years experience of creating 'moments of change' with organisations and individuals, espcially those supporting Children & Young People, to the table.
