KindleCo works in the interests of Children, Young People & Families

Founded by Jo Broad in 2004, KindleCo is a specialist partner to Public Sector and Social Purpose organisations in their role as sector champions

Specialist in Strategy, Brokerage, Project inception & Partnership development, Jo provides a 'best friend' to Organisations and Consortia occupied with stakeholder and sector-level change

This website provides a taster of the work Jo delivers to aid the efforts of the amazing workforce who support our nation's Young People.

KindleCo consults & advises on practical Strategy formulation- the design, positioning, funding, sustainability and direct brokerage of initiatives which grow the lifechances of

Children, Young People & Families -  and their network of supporting professionals

Helping organisations to understand the landscape external to their projects, strengthen their capabilities in strategy formulation, improve sector navigation and increase change-proofing.
