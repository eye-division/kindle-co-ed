KindleCo's mission is to support & enable those working with social, educational and civic purpose to reach their goals & thrive

As a specialist partner to champions of Children, Young People & Families since 2004, KindleCo works to enable Organisational, Programme, Partnership and personal development with those passionate about collaboration and are striving for social change.

Established by me, Jo Broad , KindleCo provides specialist consultancy, project management and individual coaching to organisations, stand-alone projects, professionals and to parent-carers all of whom must deliver support for Children, Young People & Young Adults against a backdrop of government-systems and public policy.
 
 

Using over 25 years of first-hand experience of coaching Children, Young People and Young Adults and of delivering 'change-maker' projects with the agencies that support them, I understand what drives this community.

I understand the challenges our social champions face.

If you are the CEO of a small charity striving to reconcile the internal needs of your organisation with the bigger picture outside....

Or a Project Manager with a complex stakeholder relationships to align....

Or a parent-carer advocating for a child....

I can connect with the challenges you face in getting the best for you & your stakeholders on a very real level.....

I bring expert knowledge & insight to the table to aid opportunity - spotting and future-proofing in your organisation. Whilst for private individuals, we will build your confidence and capabilities in navigating the system. 

Whether working with you as a Consultant or Coach, I will bring compassion and challenge to our shared task, and enable you to find your own best way forward.

Four professional values, drive all my work: 

Compassion, Collaboration, Courage, Creativity
