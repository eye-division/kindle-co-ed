KindleCo's mission is to support our champions of Children, Young People & Families to reach their goals & thrive!

Specialist partner for Organisational, Project, Partnerships and Personal development to organisations & individuals working with social purpose

Established by me, Jo Broad  in 2004, KindleCo provides specialist consultancy, project management and individual coaching to organisations, projects, professionals and parent-carers who must deliver support for Children & Young People against a backdrop of government-systems and public policy.
 
 

Using over 25 years of first-hand experience of coaching Children & Young People themselves, and of delivering large-scale projects with the agencies that support them, I understand what drives the workforce and the organisational challenges our children's champions face.

Perhaps you are a CEO of a small charity who needs to reconcile the internal needs of your organisation with the bigger picture outside ?

Or a Project Manager with a complex stakeholder relationships you must align ?

I can connect with the challenges you and your stakeholders face in getting the best for your Children & Young People on a very real level.....

I bring expert knowledge and insight to the table to aid opportunity - spotting and managing risk to your project ideas.

Together we will build your organisation's vocabulary, confidence and capabilities in navigating the external landscape and plot a course through the ever-changing eco system 

Not an organisation? Don't go away.... 

I also work with individuals, through Coaching!

Open to professionals and parent-carers

If you are directly supporting vulnerable children & young people then you may have experienced overload, be it human or systems-generated! Reconciling your own needs and aspirations, whilst enabling others to meet theirs, can be tough. 

As a Coach, with insight borne of experience, I will strike the right balance between compassion and challenge in our work together, to help you find your own best way forward.
