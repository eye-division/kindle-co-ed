Working in the interests of Children, Young People & Families

 

KindleCo is an independent, specialist provider of Consultancy, Coaching & Programme Leadership for Social Purpose organisations and the Public Sector.

Founded by Jo Broad in 2004, KindleCo specialises in Strategy & Development, enabling Organisations, Sector Partnerships,  Projects and individuals to flourish in the key Public Policy areas impacting Children, Young People & Families.

With more than 25 years experience of establishing new initiatives in, with and through the Public Sector, Jo is passionate about the power of collaboration, the value of systems-level thinking, the importance of equality of opportunity and the impact of creativity.

KindleCo consults & advises on Strategy, and on the design, positioning, funding, sustainability and brokerage of initiatives which grow the lifechances of Children & Young People. 

Jo specialises in supporting the sector champions of

  • Social Inclusion
  • Learning, Education & Skills
  • Mental Health & Wellbeing
  • the Arts

KindleCo is values - led and outcomes – driven:

Collaboration    Compassion      Courage     Creativity

I will work with you to enable impact & resilience to be built into your work streams, your organisation and your contribution to the sector.

Collaborative endeavour is my rocket fuel and I want it to be yours too!

  • respond confidently to sector change
  • plan strategically
  • build capacity
  • design new services
  • broker strategic alliances & partnerships
  • develop stakeholder networks
  • deliver multi-agency projects
  • enrich & underpin organisational development
  • lead with and through others

 

Jo understands the ethos of organisations that innovate and knows how to deliver results
