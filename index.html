KindleCo's mission

To enable the champions of children, young people and their supporting adults, to master their external landscape, and thrive 

Established by Jo Broad, KindleCo has been a specialist business partner, coach & mentor to champions of Children, Young People and their supporting adults, since 2004
 
I bring 25+ years experience to the table of navigating the systems, strategies and public policy surrounding Children & Young People and their care-givers, to build exciting projects, develop organisational strategy and support individuals working in the sector.
 
I am an experienced multi-agency projects manager, fluent in leading strategic & 'change-maker' projects.
I am highly experienced in developing stakeholder relations including work which brings together trained & lived expereince and deploy first-hand experience of working with Children, Young People and Adults to help inform my work.
 
If you are in the business of developing evidence-based projects, programmes or strategic partnerships, I can support you with expert, empathetic Consultancy and Project Management support.
