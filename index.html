Working in the interests of Children, Young People & Families

KindleCo is an independent, specialist provider of Consultancy, Coaching & Programme Leadership 

for Social Purpose organisations and the Public Sector.

 

Founded by Jo Broad in 2004, KindleCo specialises in Strategy & Development - the enablement of: 

  Organisations

   Sector Partnerships

   Projects, cross-disciplinary & multi-agency

   Networks

   Professionals 

KindleCo consults & advises on practical Strategy formulation- the design, positioning, funding, sustainability of initiatives which grow the lifechances of

Children, Young People, Families and their network of supporting professionals 

With more than 25 years experience of establishing new initiatives in, with and through the Public Sector, Jo is passionate about the power of collaboration, the value of systems-level thinking, the importance of equality of opportunity and the positive impact of creativity.

Helping Leaders and their Teams to build shared perspective, understand the landscape external to their projects and strengthen their organisational capabilities in strategy formulation, sector navigation, policy response and change-proofing.

KindleCo supports the key Public Policy areas impacting Children, Young People & Families by

Supporting sector champions of:

Social Inclusion

Learning, Education & Skills

Mental Health & Wellbeing

Co Production

the Arts

To respond confidently to sector change, plan creatively & strategically, build meaningful relationships, create exciting projects, deliver impact and remain sustainable.

KindleCo is values - led and outcomes – driven, committing to work with:

Collaboration   Compassion    Courage   Creativity

I will work with you to enable greater impact & resilience to be built into your work streams, your organisation and your contribution to the sector

If you need want to drive forward your strategic development, get in touch for an exploratory chat

Your project does not need to be large-scale. You just need to be ambitious with & for Children, Young People, Families and their workforce.

Collaborative endeavour is my rocket fuel and I want it to be yours too!​​​​​​.....
