KindleCo's mission is to enable all those working in the interests of children, young people, families and their stakeholder communities, to thrive 

Established by me, Jo Broad, in 2004,  KindleCo is a specialist business partner and coach to the champions of Children, Young People & Families
 
Through Consultancy, Project Management, Coaching & Mentoring, I support & enable the development of Organisations, Projects, strategic Partnerships and People, including parents
 
For all those working with the systems, strategies and public policy surrounding Children & Young People, as an organisation or an individual, I bring 25+ years experience to the table of navigating this environment
 
Deploying first-hand experience of coaching Children, Young People and of leading 'change-maker' projects with the agencies that support them, I understand what drives this community
 
The support you give to your beneficiaries, or the individual children you are advocating for, has to scan the horizon and deal with the present to have best chance of real impact
 
I enable clients, addressing organisational or individual challenges, allied to supporting young people, to sharpen their strategy and navigate a way forward which is well-informed and sustainable
 
 

Four professional values, drive all my work: 

Compassion, Collaboration, Courage, Creativity 
You may be the CEO of a charity striving to reconcile the internal needs of your organisation with the bigger picture outside....or a Project Manager with a complex stakeholder relationships to align....

 

Perhaps you're a parent-carer bamboozled by the system whilst trying to advocate for your child....?

Together, we will build your capabilities

This site is an online portfolio but not a sales site.  Please browse my case studies . I welcome informal chats. I offer a free, no- obligation 30 minute phone or video call, to explore how we might work together
