KindleCo's mission is to enable those working with social, educational and civic purpose to thrive in a landscape of change

As a specialist partner to champions of Children, Young People & Families since 2004, KindleCo enables Organisational, Programme, Partnership and Personal development for anyone working with systems, strategy and public policy surrounding Children & Young People.
 
Established by me, Jo Broad, KindleCo provides specialist consultancy, project management, individual coaching & mentoring
 
My clients are organisations, strategic partnerships, stand-alone projects, individual professionals and parent-carers
 
Over the past 17 years, I have enabled clients, with tasks of all shapes and sizes, to navigate a way forward, hone their strategy and sustain their own support for beneficiaries, stakeholders, their careers and  their families. 
 
 

Four professional values, drive all my work: 

Compassion, Collaboration, Courage, Creativity 

Using over 25 years of first-hand experience of coaching Children, Young People & Young Adults, and leading 'change-maker' projects with the agencies that support them, I understand what drives this professional community.

You may be the CEO of a charity striving to reconcile the internal needs of your organisation with the bigger picture outside....

Or a Project Manager with a complex stakeholder relationships to align....

I will bring expert knowledge & insight to the table to aid you in opportunity - spotting and future - proofing for your organisation.

Or perhaps you're a parent-carer bamboozled by the system whilst trying to advocate for your child....?

Together, we will build your confidence & capability in understanding the external landscape and navigating the system.

Whether as Consultant, Project Manager, Coach or Mentor I will bring compassion & challenge to our shared task, enabling you and your stakeholders to find your best way forward
