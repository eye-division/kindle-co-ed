KindleCo specialises in Strategy & Development for Social Purpose Organisations & Public Sector

Working with Organisations, Sector Partnerships, Projects & Professionals in key Public Policy areas impacting  Children, Young People & Families

KindleCo's founder and Lead Consultant is Jo Broad who supports sector champions through:

 

organisational Consultancy

project Coaching

programme Leadership

individual Surgeries

 

To:

respond confidently to sector change

plan strategically

build capacity

broker alliances

design new work streams

deepen stakeholder relationships

deliver multi-agency projects

enrich organisational development

lead with through & for others

 

Whose mission is to promote:

Social Inclusion

Learning, Education & Workforce Skills

Mental Health & Wellbeing

Co Production

the Arts

 

Work together in one of three ways or as part of a bespoke blended offer:

Consultancy

working alongside organisational Leaders & Directors of multi-agency programmes

Where you need greater shared purpose, objectives, roles, language & outcomes: consultancy input will support the planning, design and partnership development within mixed partnerships, especially suited to those aiming for sector-level or geographical impact.

Builds infrastructure Provides project oversight

 

Project Coaching

with multi-agency projects & partnerships Leads & their Teams

Where you need to mobilise diverse interests around a specific agenda or action point: coaching key team members and even your stakeholder groups can help to sharpen the shared purpose that drives collaboration and reconcile parties around specific aspects of policy, strategy, funding and stakeholder buy-in. 

Addresses barriers to effective inter-agency delivery. Builds project resilience

 

Inception Advice & Information Surgeries

for individual start-up projects, and for professionals

Where project initiators need to understand the 'bigger picture': can support you, and your owm clients through an injection of  targeted inception advice,  with a timely audit of your project’s capacity to navigate the public sector landscape, with its opportunities and pitfalls

Informs financial planning & next steps

For individual professionals preparing for a next-step career moment:  including pre-Publication editorial and pre-Interview thinking
