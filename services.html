Parents / Carers

Are you directly supporting & advocating for a young person with additional challenges, complex needs or vulnerabilities?  

I work with:

 carers

 parents, grandparents, family members leading on child support

advocates

 practitioners 

I work individually with children's advocates of all sorts. My aim is to help you find your own best way forward, building on your strengths. Supporting vulnerable young people can so often feel like a quagmire of systems-barriers and emotional treacle where your own wellbeing can get left behind.

returning-to-work or change of direction 

following sabbatical, career or carer-break

 preparation surgeries

to support you before key meetings & through systems challenges

I provide a safe space for non-judgmental, confidential, coaching-orientated conversations

Contact me in complete confidence

What I do not offer: I am not a psychologist or clinician and I do not offer any medical advice. My sessions are not counselling. All information is treated in upmost confidence. To keep my clients safe, if during the course of our work together, I have a safeguarding concern about you, or your child's wellbeing, I will try to explore this with you within the bounds of good safeguarding practice and reach a decision on how you may be referred onwards, to an appropriate source of professional help.
