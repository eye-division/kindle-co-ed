Be the champion of your own future!

I specialise in supporting & enabling clients to positively navigate change, through:

 

Coaching 1:1

(please note: this includes, but is NOT exclusive to, parents & carers)
 

Consultancy

for organisations working with children, young people & families
 

Mentoring

for project teams & private individuals

 

I will help and guide you to:

stay connected to your core values, innate talents, purpose & abilities

develop strategies which enable you to embrace change, so it becomes part of your roadmap, and less of a dead end

 nurture self-awareness & deal constructively with limiting beliefs

 be ready to confidently step forward into a self-determined plan of action

with you holding the reigns of change

I will work with you to make this time of change, not just survivable, but thriveable

Parents / Carers

Are you directly supporting & advocating for a person with additional challenges, needs or vulnerabilities?  

I work with:

 carers

 parents, grandparents, family members leading on child support

advocates

 practitioners of vocational occupations including Musicians & the Arts 

I work individually with children's advocates of all sorts. My aim is to help you find your own best way forward, building on your strengths. Supporting vulnerable young people can so often feel like a quagmire of systems-barriers and emotional treacle where your own wellbeing can get left behind.

returning-to-work, change of direction, medical discharge & forced career endings 

following sabbaticals, career change & endings, caring responsibilities

 advice surgeries

to support you before key meetings & through systems challenges

I provide a safe space for non-judgmental, confidential, coaching-orientated conversations

Contact me in complete confidence

What I do not offer: I am not a psychologist or clinician and I do not offer any medical advice. My sessions are not counselling. All information is treated in upmost confidence. To keep my clients safe, if during the course of our work together, I have a safeguarding concern about you, or your child's wellbeing, I will try to explore this with you within the bounds of good safeguarding practice and reach a decision on how you may be referred onwards, to an appropriate source of professional help.
