What makes KindleCo different to other Consultancies ?

My work is policy-informed, practice- sensitive and purpose-driven

Who do I work with ?

Children's champions can be found in every walk of life. Champions grow out of Professional Practice, and out of Lived Experience. Some work in organisations, some work at home and others do unpaid work as carers.

I support & enable both sector, and individual, champions of Children, Young People & Families regardless of occupation, title or public profile.

KindleCo brings a seasoned blend of competencies its work with organisations and private individuals.

I draw on my own professional practice & personal insights built over more than 25 years of work with, for and alongside children, young people, families and their champions

If you are an organisation, please give me a call to discuss work on any of the following themes:

Social Inclusion, Equality of opportunity

Lifelong Learning, Education, Skills development

Mental Health, Wellbeing, Vulnerable families

Creativity, Creative Industries, the Arts
