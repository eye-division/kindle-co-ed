Children's champions can be found in many walks of life

They may grow out of professional practice, or, out of Lived Experience

Some work in organisations, some work independently. Some do unpaid work as carers or are passionate advocates

Champions create ripples of systems-change regardless of where they are 

I support and enable sector & private individual champions

Through Consultancy, Project Management, Coaching & Mentoring

I support those navigating:

Social Inclusion

Community capacity-building

Education, Learning & Skills

Trauma support & Vulnerable families

Wellbeing including Mental Health

Young People in the Arts & Creative Industries

What makes KindleCo different  ?

Our work together will be 

policy-informed, practice - sensitive , purpose -driven

I will draw on my own professional practice & personal insights built over more than 25 years of work for (and alongside) children, young people, parents, families and their champions to help you build on your many strengths

Lets make navigating this arena not just survivable, but thriveable!

Parents / Carers

Are you directly supporting or advocating for a young person with additional challenges, complex needs or vulnerabilities?  

I work with:

 carers

 parents, grandparents, family members leading on child support

advocates

 practitioners 

I work individually with children's advocates of all sorts. My aim is to help you find your own best way forward, building on your strengths. Supporting vulnerable young people can so often feel like a quagmire of systems-barriers and emotional treacle where your own wellbeing can get left behind.

Clients can choose their focus, examples include:

return-to-work sabbatical, career or carer-break

job interview preparation in fields related to children & families

 preparation for meetings reviews, systems challenges

a safe space for non-judgmental, confidential conversations

I offer:

Coaching, Mentoring & Surgeries depending on the task

I aim to make sessions as accessible financially as possible for carers. I also offer taster sessions and one-off 'surgery' formats, prior to embarking on any larger series of work together

Contact me in complete confidence

What I do not offer: I am not a psychologist or clinician and I do not offer any medical advice. My sessions are not counselling. All information is treated in upmost confidence. To keep my clients safe, if during the course of our work together, I have a safeguarding concern about you, or your child's wellbeing, I will try to explore this with you and reach a decision on how you may be referred onwards, to an appropriate source of professional help.
