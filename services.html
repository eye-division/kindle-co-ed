What makes KindleCo different to other Consultancies?

My work is policy-informed, practice- sensitive and purpose-driven

Download the sample KindleCo Services Flyer 

KindleCo brings a seasoned blend of competencies its work with organisations and private individuals. I draw on my own professional practice & personal insights built over more than 25 years of work with, for and around children, and their champions

Children's champions can be found in every walk of life and grow not only out of professional practice, but out of Lived Experience. 

I aid, support and enable both sector and individual champions of Children, Young People & Families wherever they exist and regardless of public profile.

I strive to use my experience to help those champions, so they may continue to deliver vital and joyful opportunities for our young people to be:

- included, safe, healthy, skilled, creative, ambitious, agents of change

Please give me a call to discuss any of the following:

Social Inclusion, Equality of opportunity

Lifelong Learning, Education, Skills development

Mental Health, Wellbeing, Vulnerable families

Creativity, Creative Industries, the Arts
