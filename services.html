For the Public Sector & its Social Purpose partners the need to know your external landscape, apply systems-level thinking, collaborate across traditional agency lines, share decision-making with others and shape local solutions in context of the bigger picture, has never been greater.

It can feel overwhelming....but it's also inspiring and full of opportunity....

Through KindleCo, I bring a unique blend of competencies to aid the sector champions of our Children, Young People & Families so that they can continue to deliver vital and joyful opportunities for our young people to be included, learn & develop new skills, improve their wellbeing, experience creativity

I deliver:

Consultancy - for organisational, partnerships & projects delivering in a public policy context especially cross-boundary initiatives

 Coaching - for multi-agency projects, partnerships & stakeholder groups

Programme Leadership & Project Management - for strategic initiatives delivering in context of government influenced systems, policy, strategy

Surgeries - for Public Sector & Social Purpose Projects  / Professionals including #return-to-work  #Carers  working in Education, Health and the Arts

What makes KindleCo different to other Consultancies? 

I strive to bring knowledge, insights and ideas borne out of experience at the coal face of practice and project delivery, to the table. My input will be policy-informed & practice-sensitive 

I am at my most effective, creative & professionally passionate partnering in the Public Policy areas of:

Social Inclusion & Equality of opportunity

Lifelong Learning, Education & Skills

Mental Health & Wellbeing, vulnerable CYP

Creativity & the Arts
