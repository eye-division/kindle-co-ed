For the Public Sector & its Social Purpose partners the need to know your external landscape, apply systems-level thinking, collaborate across traditional agency lines, share decision-making with others and shape local solutions in context of the bigger picture, has never been greater.

It can feel overwhelming....but it's also inspiring and full of opportunity....

Through KindleCo, I bring a unique blend of competencies and personal insights built over 25 years, to aid the sector champions of our Children, Young People & Families so that they can continue to deliver vital and joyful opportunities for our young people to be included, stay safe, learn & develop new skills, improve wellbeing and experience creativity.

I believe that, every child should benefit from compassionate, accessible systems built on an eco-system of diverse, sustainable organisations who collaborate for the greater good

Programme leadership - for multi-stakeholder  projects

What makes KindleCo different to other Consultancies? 

I strive to bring expertise and ideas, borne of experience at the coal face,  to the table. My input will be policy-informed, practice-sensitive and bring 'expert-by-experience' compassion to our work.

I am at my most effective, creative & professionally passionate partnering in the Public Policy areas of:

Social Inclusion, Equality of opportunity

Lifelong Learning, Education, Skills development

Mental Health, Wellbeing, Vulnerable families

Creativity, Creative Industries business support, the Arts
