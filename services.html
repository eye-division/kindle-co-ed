If you are already working in this arena, your passion for using your work, to drive change for others, will already be within you.

You won't need me to nurture that. But you might be feeling a mix of emotions.

It can feel overwhelming right?
The external landscape is inspiring and full of opportunity and a few obstacles along the way........

Through KindleCo, I bring a unique blend of competencies and personal insights built over 25 years, to aid the sector champions of our Children, Young People & Young Adults so that they can continue to deliver vital and joyful opportunities for our young people to be included, stay safe, learn & develop new skills, improve wellbeing and drive creativity.

Using that experience, I now partner organisations and individuals working with Social, Educational and Civic purpose across a range of intersecting policy areas to build their confidence & capacity, whilst they capacity-build others.

I believe in the power of compassionate, accessible systems built on an eco-system of diverse, sustainable organisations and individuals who collaborate for the greater good
