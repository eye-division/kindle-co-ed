Your privacy, and your experience of using this site is important to me. 

This website is an on-line portfolio for Jo Broad and KindleCo. The site is not used to sell any goods or services directly.

It additionally offers the visitor some links to published resources. These are available to read or download for free. Where you go to a third party source of information, KindleCo is not responsible for the content or accuracy of external sites.

If you use our online contact form via this website, we will only store the information you supply to respond to your enquiry. Your details will never be passed to a third party without your permission. We do not currently publish a newsletter or undertake mailshots.

As a very small enterprise, every effort is made to keep your personal information and data secure.

You can contact us, via this website, at any time to ask to have any details you submit through it, removed from our records.

Thank you.

Cookies

This site uses cookies to help us continue to improve visitor experience. If you use this site, we’ll assume you’re OK with this.

We use Google Analytics to compile anonymous, aggregated statistics that allow us to understand how users use the website and to help us improve it. Google sets a cookie for this purpose. We do not use tracking cookies to record web sites you have previously visited.
