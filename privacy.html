Your privacy, and your experience of using this site is important to me. 

This website is an on-line portfolio for Jo Broad and KindleCo. It additionally offers insights, links published resources, accessible through it. These are available to read or download for free.

The site is not used to sell any goods or services directly. 

If you use our online contact form via this website, we will only store the information you supply to respond to your enquiry, where appropriate.

As a very small enterprise, every effort is made to keep your data secure and it will never be knowingly passed to a third party without your explicit permission.

You can contact us, via this website, at any time to ask to have your details removed from our database.

Thank you.

Cookies

This site uses cookies to help us continue to improve visitor experience. If you use this site, we’ll assume you’re OK with this.

We use Google Analytics to compile anonymous, aggregated statistics that allow us to understand how users use the website and to help us improve it. Google sets a cookie for this purpose. We do not use tracking cookies to record web sites you have previously visited.
